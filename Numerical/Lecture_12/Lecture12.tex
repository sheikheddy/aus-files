\section*{Lagrange Polynomial Error Function}

\[ E(x) = |f(x) - P_n(x)| = \frac{|(x-x_0)(x-x_1)\dots(x-x_n)f^{n+1}(\xi)|}{(n+1)!}  \]

Where $ \xi  $ is the element in the smallest interval containing $ x_0,x_1,\dots,x_n $
